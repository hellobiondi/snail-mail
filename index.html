What is Snail Mail?

An online social networking portal that enables our users to connect with new people, both locally and internationally, through a virtual simulated mail and parcel network. Created to counter the lack of social interactions during this Pandemic, Snail Mail matches you to completely random people who are as equally interested as you when it comes to meeting new people, thus promoting diverse connections.

Different Users, Different Conversations

Snail Mail aims to improve social interactions among our users. Hence, with our application, you can be assured that every new user you match with is unique. This will therefore promote diversity among the different people you meet through our application.

Have A Chat Or A Game, It's Up To You

Apart from having a friendly conversation with a new friend you meet, how about engaging in a couple of friendly games to break the ice?

Here at Snail Mail, we have an wide array of games for you to choose from. Make a move, send it as a mail, and wait for your friend to make his or hers, it's as simple as that!
