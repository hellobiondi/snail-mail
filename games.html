Connect 4

A two-player connection board game, in which the players choose a color and then take turns dropping colored discs into a seven-column, six-row vertically suspended grid.

The first player to connect 4 in a row is the winner of the game.

Hangman

OA guessing gam where one player thinks of a word, phrase or sentence and the other(s) tries to guess it by suggesting letters within a certain number of guesses.

The game ends when guessing player completes/guess the word or when the other player completes the diagram of the hanged man.
